Venison Stew

Description

This rich and filling stew is a staple among Skyrim’s hunters. Packed with protein and warmth — perfect after trekking through the snowy tundra.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb venison (cubed)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • 1 potato (diced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and brown the venison pieces.
  2. Add onion, cook until soft.
  3. Add potatoes and carrots, stir for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in beef broth, add thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and cover.
  6. Simmer for 1–1.5 hours until meat is tender and flavors meld.
