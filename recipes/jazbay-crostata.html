Jazbay Crostata

Description

A delightful dessert made with the elusive Jazbay Grapes. Tangy and sweet with a buttery crust — this crostata is a favorite among alchemists and sweet-toothed Nords alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust (homemade or store-bought)
  • 2 cups Jazbay grapes (or red grapes as substitute)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine grapes, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla.
  3. Roll out the pie crust onto a baking sheet.
  4. Spoon the grape mixture into the center, leaving a 2-inch border.
  5. Fold the edges over the filling, pleating as needed.
  6. Brush crust with egg wash for a golden finish.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes until crust is golden and filling is bubbly.
