Apple Dumpling

A simple yet delicious snack found throughout Skyrim. These sweets are like bite-sized Apple Pies often enjoyed in homes. While they are hardly enough for an end-of-meal dessert Apple Dumplings serve as an excellent treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet puff pastry sheets
  • 6 large apples (peeled & diced)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 egg (for brushing)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200C/392F. Roll out your pastry and cut into 5" squares. Grease a baking tray well and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine your diced apples, spices, sugar, vanilla essence, and half the butter. Mix well, and fill your pastries with a spoon. Fold the pastries into the centre at all four corners and pinch together in the centre with wet fingers to keep the pastry together.
  3. Separate the dumplings on the baking tray so they do not stick together. Brush your dumplings with the remaining butter and egg glaze, and bake for 30-45 minutes, or until well browned. Remove from the oven to cool. Serve plain or drizzle with maple syrup.
