Apple Dumpling

A simple yet delicious snack found throughout Skyrim. These sweets are like bite-sized Apple Pies often enjoyed in homes. While they are hardly enough for an end-of-meal dessert Apple Dumplings serve as an excellent treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet puff pastry sheets
  • 6 large apples (peeled & diced)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 egg (for brushing)
