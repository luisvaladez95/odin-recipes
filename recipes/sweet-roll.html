Sweet Roll

Description

A classic Skyrim pastry topped with sugary icing. Guard it well... or someone might steal it.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix wet and dry ingredients separately, then combine.
  3. Pour batter into greased molds and bake for 20–25 minutes.
  4. Let cool, then drizzle icing on top.
