Sweet Roll

The most iconic dessert in all of Skyrim. Time and time again players have heard the famous line, "Let me guess...someone stole your Sweetroll?". While this line is said sarcastically it is not surprising to think someone would steal this delectable dessert.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 3 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 2 vanilla pod (scraped)
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 cup icing sugar
